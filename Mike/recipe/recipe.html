Barbados is a beautiful island in the Caribbean, known for its stunning beaches, vibrant culture, and delicious cuisine. One of the most popular dishes in Barbados is corn pie, a savory and sweet dish that is loved by locals and visitors alike.

One of the most well known and favorited side dishes in Barbados is corn pie. You can find corn pie at just about any Barbadian family gathering or lunch. It is considered an easy, go to dish which everyone loves and no one can resist.

It is a dish that is often served with spicy foods such as pepperpot, macaroni pie, fried chicken, and even fish cakes. Corn pie is a dish that is made with corn, eggs, milk, cheese, and onions. It is baked in the oven until it is golden brown and set.

The sweet and buttery flavour of corn pie couples perfectly with the spicy foods and presents a sort of cooling to your pallete, and best of all it takes ten minutes tops to prepare! Take a look below for our simple, easy to do recipe for Bajan Corn Pie.


CORN PIE RECIPE INGREDIENTS

  • 1 can corn
  • 1 can cream style corn
  • 2 tablespoons grated onion
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 3 tablespoons melted buter
  • 2 ozs milk
  • 2 eggs well beaten
  • 2 ozs. grated cheese
  • 2 tablespoons (heaped) flour

CORN PIE RECIPE INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees

  2. In a bowl, Mix all ingredients together other than the beaten eggs, fold those in last.

  3. Place the mixture into a greased pie plate or a 9‚Äù square pan.

  4. Bake for 35-40 minutes until the mixture is set. Enjoy!
