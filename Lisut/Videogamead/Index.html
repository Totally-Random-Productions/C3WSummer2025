About Infinity Nikki

Infinity Nikki is a free-to-play cozy-adventure game with dress-up elements developed and published by Papergames (with publishing outside mainland China under Infold Games), which released for Android, iOS, PlayStation 5, and PC on 5 December 2024. It is the fifth installment in the Nikki series and a sequel to Shining Nikki. The game features open world exploration, platforming, and puzzle-solving.

Set in the world of Miraland, players accompany Nikki and her feline companion, Momo, on a quest to rescue a mystical god of wishes. Different outfits grant abilities for navigating diverse environments and solving challenges.

Who is Nikki & Momo?

Nikki who traverses time and space and the person you play as and dress up as you explore past Miraland trying to prevent the destruction of Miraland in the future by rewriting the Miraland's destiny. Beside her is Momo a talking cat who loves BBQ and can mystriously track whimstars.

Wardobe

Wardrobe is a Game System in Infinity Nikki where the player can create different looks, and can also view all owned clothing pieces.

Sea of Stars

The Sea of stars is a transcendent, hyper-dimensional realm where past, present and future intertwine.

Swan Gazebo

The Swan Gazebo is located in Breezy Meadow in Infinity Nikki. Not only is this spot a gorgeous space for photos, but it's also home to a unique crafting resource.

Mira Crown

Mira Crown is the arena Styling Contest in Infinity Nikki.

Resonance

Resonance in Infinity Nikki are the Banners where you exchange high-tier in-game currencies called Revelation Crystals and Resonite Crystals for random rewards to get your hands on limited-time or high-level outfit pieces to add to your wardrobe.
