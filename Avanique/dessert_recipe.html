Ingredients

Oreo Crust Essentials

  • 24 Oreo Cookies
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted

White Chocolate Cheesecake Filling

  • 3 bricks (24 ounces) full-fat cream cheese, VERY soft
  • 1 cup (8 ounces) full fat sour cream, at room temperature
  • 3 large eggs + 2 egg yolks, at room temperature
  • 1 and 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice
  • 8 ounces white chocolate, chopped, melted and slightly cooled

Instructions

Prepare the pan

  1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees (F). Wrap a 9" springform pan VERY well in multiple layers of heavy-duty tin foil; lightly grease the pan with non-stick spray, and set aside.
  2. Beat the softened cream cheese and sour cream in a blender until completely smooth, scraping the sides and bottom of the container as needed. You want this mixture completely smooth before adding anymore ingredients!
  3. Add in the eggs, egg yolks, and sugar and pulse until combined. Add in the vanilla and pulse for another 10 seconds. Don't over mix!
  4. Using a rubber spatula, fold in the lemon juice, then fold in the melted white chocolate.
  5. Pour the filling into the prepared crust, spread evenly, and smooth the top with a rubber spatula.
  6. Top with the raspberry swirl.
  7. Place the springform pan into a large baking pan (with high sides) and fill the pan halfway with hot water; this is your water bath (see post if you need more information on this).
