Teen Wolf

Picture showing the show

By Jeff Davis is the combination of supernatural elements with the trials and tribulations of high school life. The show follows a teenage boy named Scott McCall who is bitten by a werewolf and subsequently discovers a world of supernatural creatures, while also navigating the challenges of growing up and forming relationships. The series hooks viewers with its mix of action, drama, and coming-of-age themes, as well as its unique take on the werewolf mythology.
