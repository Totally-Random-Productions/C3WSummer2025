Glorbaxian Slime Pods

A truly delectable treat, famous on planet Xylar for its unique texture and zesty, bio-luminescent glow. A favorite among young Glorbaxians!

Ingredients

  • One dozen freshly harvested Flar-Flar eggs
  • A single, pulsating Nebula Nodule
  • Three cups of dehydrated Martian moon-dust
  • A splash of Venusian swamp gas (for flavor)
  • A pinch of crystallized asteroid salt
  • Gaseous Glimmer Worms (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Gently crack the Flar-Flar eggs into a non-reactive silicon bowl. The yolks should be a shimmering, iridescent color. If they are merely glowing, they are over-ripe.
  2. Carefully dice the Nebula Nodule. BE WARNED: It will attempt to phase in and out of this dimension. Use a temporal-stasis field to keep it stable.
  3. Whisk the diced nodule into the eggs. The mixture should begin to hum at a low frequency.
  4. Slowly fold in the dehydrated moon-dust. The mixture will thicken and begin to form semi-sentient pods. Do not be alarmed by their soft cooing noises.
  5. Just before serving, introduce the Venusian swamp gas. This will activate the bio-luminescence.
  6. Arrange the pods on a platter and sprinkle with asteroid salt.

Serving Suggestion

For a truly authentic experience, release the Gaseous Glimmer Worms just above the platter and allow them to float down onto the pods. Serve immediately. Best enjoyed in a zero-gravity environment.
